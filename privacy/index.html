Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ToonTone Cats is a free browser-based game. We take your privacy seriously.

What We Collect

We collect nothing. ToonTone Cats runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no signups, no tracking cookies are used by us.

Your game progress (best score, games played) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

We display ads via Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve personalized ads. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
