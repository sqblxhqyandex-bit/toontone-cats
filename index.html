FAQ

What is MeowGuess?

MeowGuess is a free browser-based cat toon tone color memory game. It's like Toon Tone with cats! Each round shows you an original cat character, challenges you to memorize a specific color from their design, then asks you to recreate it using HSB (Hue, Saturation, Brightness) sliders. It's a fun way to test how well you perceive and remember color — with cats!

Is MeowGuess like Toon Tone?

Yes! MeowGuess is exactly the same type of game as Toon Tone — a cartoon color memory challenge — but with an all-cast cast of 12 original cat characters. If you enjoy Toon Tone Daily, you'll love our cat-themed version.

Is MeowGuess free?

Yes, completely free. No account, no signup, no payment. Just open your browser and play.

How is the score calculated?

Each round is scored from 0.00 to 10.00 based on the difference between your guess and the target color in RGB space. The closer your color, the higher your score. A perfect 10.00 means you matched it exactly. Using the hint feature subtracts 1 point from that round.

How many rounds per game?

Each game has 5 rounds. Your final score is the average of all 5 rounds. A 9.00+ average means you have an excellent color memory!

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! MeowGuess is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop browsers.

Where do the cats come from?

All cats in MeowGuess are original hand-drawn SVG characters — independent art and lovingly crafted. They are not from any real game or cartoon.
